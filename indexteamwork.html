ATBS

The best or nothing

This product integrates ATBS vehicle protocol decoding chip with TPMS, and is equipped with Bluetooth transmission interface. Moreover, it is the most compact and versatile mini OBD2 decoder in the world! When a smart phone is equipped with a different APP, it will turn the phone into either a diagnostic tool or a black box, depending on the APP. It is able to rate user’s driving behavior and it can also send information and position for rescue purpose. Furthermore, it is a new power management system as well. By using a mobile phone, user can monitor tire pressure and temperature, and OBD2 information, such as battery voltage, speed, vehicle’s fuel consumption, driving record, in order to achieve fuel-efficient driving and carbon emission reduction.
