Company History

The Beginning of ATBS

In 2006, ATBS was founded and released TPMS (Tire Pressure Monitoring System). Next year, TPMS was adopted by Taiwan MaoKong gondola. One terminal host can receives signals from 1000 wheels. Afterward, we signed a contract of technology transfer with listed company. Assisted the listed company to develop TPMS product. Successfully introduced the product into body shop. As the variety of products increases, we start to provide a wide range of OEM and ODM service.

2006 - 2009

Apple MFi Licensee Granted

ATBS released a short of interpreters and vehicle decoding modules in 2010. Integrated with vehicles, 2DIN machine and navigation machine industry and it supports more than 5000 different kinds of vehicles, including passenger cars and trucks. 2011 was a big leap for ATBS since not only did ATBS develop a sort of Interpreter Dongle / Head Up Display(HUD) some of them acquired to be standard equipment of lots of brand of cars, but ATBS received Apple MFi License which shows the quality of ATBS was already approved by Apple Inc. as well.

2010 - 2013

Since 2006, products of ATBS have been nominated as innovation award lasting for 8 years. Especially in recent years, ATBS has started to win more and more prizes in Taipei AMPA. OBD2 Solution

ATBS OBD2 Solution reads and interprets the data and driving information from a wide range of vehicle. Depending on the requirments from customers, ATBS can get a dozen of parameters including RPM, speed and so on from the ECU in vehicles to do further analysis and correct the driving behavior. With the iOS and Android application which ATBS developed called "Saving Fuel", the drivers can know the vehicle condition in the real time. Not only will it increase the safety but reduce the cost of maintenance the vehicle as well.

HUD Solution

ATBS HUD solution is the extension of OBD2 function. It is not only a multifunctional tool which provides drivers with important information of vehicle but also makes driving safer. The information contains vehicle speed, engine RPM, fuel consumption rate, engine loading, battery voltage, even TPMS information could be included as well. The Above information are displayed via mode selection. Speeding, low battery voltage, over coolant temperature and abnormal tire pressure will trigger warning alarm immediately when it detects the abnormal condition.

AT100G

AT100G which implements internet of vehicle(IoV) with 6 functions inside. Not only interpret the data from ECU, but it also could provides the Wifi signal by installing SIM card inside to let other devices access to internet. Besides, the condition of every single vehicle can be transmitted to control center with Internet. . As for the function of GPS function, managers are able to know more about the trace of vehicless. All in all, those functions mentioned above are very useful for fleet owner to analyze and optimize the service route, especially logistics industries and passenger carriers.
